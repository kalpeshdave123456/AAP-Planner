📌 Key Planning Assumptions & Methodology

  • 4% Withdrawal Rule: Based on the Trinity Study, starting with 4% of total corpus and increasing by inflation annually.
  • Retirement Horizon: Calculations assume a 30-year withdrawal period.
  • Returns (Mean): India Equity (12%), US Equity (10%), BAF (10%), Debt (6.5%), Gold (8%).
  • Volatility (SD): Factor in market crashes using Standard Deviation (Equity: 18-20%, BAF: 10%, Debt: 4%).
  • Inflation: Default 6% applied to annual withdrawals to maintain purchasing power.
  • Success Probability: The % of 2000 random market scenarios where the portfolio did not hit ₹0.
