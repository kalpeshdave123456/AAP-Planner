📌 Core Assumptions

  • The 4% Rule: Calculations assume a safe withdrawal rate (SWR) of 4% based on historical long-term averages.
  • Inflation: All projections assume a baseline inflation of 6% p.a. affecting future purchasing power.
  • Corpus Longevity: Designed for a retirement horizon of 30+ years without depleting the principal significantly.
  • Taxation: Figures are pre-tax; actual take-home may vary based on specific tax brackets and long-term capital gains.
  • Yield vs. Growth: Income is derived from a combination of direct dividends (InvITs/Debt) and systemic withdrawals from growth assets.
